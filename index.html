Welcome to the Istrail Laboratory

The Istrail Laboratory is a computational biology and computer science research group in the Department of Computer Science at Brown University. It is part of the Center for Computational Molecular Biology at Brown University. Sorin Istrail is the James A. and Julie N. Brown Professor of Computational and Mathematical Sciences and Professor of Computer Science at Brown University.
