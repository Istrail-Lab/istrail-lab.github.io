Romania Celebrates Sorin

Professor Sorin Istrail's contributions to science and education have been celebrated across Romania. This page documents some of these special recognitions and events.

Honorary Citizen of Iași

Professor Sorin Istrail was honored with the honorary citizenship of Iași, one of Romania's most important cultural and academic centers. This recognition celebrates his outstanding contributions to science and his role in promoting Romanian excellence globally.

Photo Gallery

Honorary Citizen of Târgu Neamț

In September 2024, Professor Sorin Istrail was awarded the honorary citizenship of Târgu Neamț, his hometown. This prestigious recognition celebrates his outstanding contributions to science and education.

Read the full article (in Romanian)

Meeting with Excellence at CSNM

The 'Ștefan cel Mare' National College (CSNM) in Târgu Neamț hosted Professor Istrail as the key guest at their 'Meeting with a Distinguished Professor' event. This special occasion provided young students with the unique opportunity to learn from and be inspired by Professor Istrail's journey.
